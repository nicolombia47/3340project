Profile Help



How Do I Make An Account?

To make an account, click on the profile icon on the top right of any page. When you go to that page, it will give you the option to log in or create an account. Click on the "create an account" button and followed the form. You will need an email address.


How Do I Log In?

To log in, click on the profile icon on the top right of any page. When you go to that page, if you aren't logged into an account already, it will give you the option to log in or create an account. To log in, you have to input the email address you used to create the account and the password associated with that account. If you don't remember your password, click on the "forgot password?" button below the log in credentials. Read the "how to change password" section for more information.

Contact Help



How To Contact A Seller?

You can contact a seller by either clicking on a seller's profile and click the "message" button or by going to one of the seller's item pages and click the "message" button there. A messaging page will appear where you can communicate to that person.


How Do I Send A Message?

Once you are on the messaging page (see 'How To Contact A Seller?' for how to find the messaging page), all you need to do is input text into the text bar and click the 'Send' button.


Where To View Messages?

To view your message history, go to your Profile and click the 'Messages' tab. From here you can see your messages with other users.
